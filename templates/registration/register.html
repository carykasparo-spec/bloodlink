Terms & Conditions

1. Eligibility
You must be at least 18 years old and in good health to register as a donor on BloodLink.

2. Health Disclosure
You agree to truthfully disclose any medical conditions including HIV/AIDS, diabetes, high blood sugar, skin diseases, or other conditions that may affect blood donation safety. Providing false information may endanger recipients.

3. Voluntary Participation
Blood donation through BloodLink is entirely voluntary. You may withdraw your availability at any time from your dashboard.

4. Privacy
Your personal data is stored securely and will never be sold or shared with third parties without your explicit consent. Contact details are only shared with verified recipients during an active blood request.

5. Platform Use
BloodLink is a platform to connect donors and recipients. We do not provide medical advice. Always consult a qualified medical professional before donating.

6. Accuracy of Information
You are responsible for keeping your profile information accurate and up to date, including blood type, availability, and contact details.

7. Amendments
BloodLink reserves the right to update these terms at any time. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.
